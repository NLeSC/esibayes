sodaVisualization3()

Standard visualization routine that creates 3 figures using sodaSubplotScreen(). The upper left figure shows a sodaPlotSeq() result, the lower left figure shows the log likelihood, while the right figure shows the marginal parameter distributions using sodaMargHist().
