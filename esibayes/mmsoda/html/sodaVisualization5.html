sodaVisualization5()

Standard visualization routine that creates 4 figures using sodaSubplotScreen(). The upper left figure shows a sodaMargHist() result, the lower left figure shows the objective score, the top right figure shows a sodaPlotGelmanRubin() result, while the bottom right figure shows a sodaMatrixOfScatter() result with typeStr set to 'par-par'.
