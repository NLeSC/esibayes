sodaVisualization2()

Standard visualization routine that creates 4 figures using sodaSubplotScreen(). The upper left figure shows a sodaMargHist() result, the lower left figure shows the objective score, the middle figure shows a sodaPlotGelmanRubin() result, while the right figure shows a sodaPlotSeq() result.
