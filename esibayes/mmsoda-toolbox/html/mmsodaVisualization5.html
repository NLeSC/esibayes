mmsodaVisualization5()

Standard visualization routine that creates 4 figures using mmsodaSubplotScreen(). The upper left figure shows a mmsodaMargHist() result, the lower left figure shows the objective score, the top right figure shows a mmsodaPlotGelmanRubin() result, while the bottom right figure shows a mmsodaMatrixOfScatter() result with typeStr set to 'par-par'.
