Description

mmsodaVisualization2()

Standard visualization routine that creates 4 figures using mmsodaSubplotScreen(). The upper left figure shows a mmsodaMargHist() result, the lower left figure shows the objective score, the middle figure shows a mmsodaPlotGelmanRubin() result, while the right figure shows a mmsodaPlotSeq() result.
